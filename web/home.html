Welcome to the Public Works Department, Kerala

The Public Works Department (PWD) of Kerala is responsible for the development, construction, and maintenance of essential infrastructure across the state, including roads, bridges, and government buildings. Dedicated to quality and sustainability, PWD Kerala supports the socio-economic growth of the state by ensuring safe and efficient connectivity for all citizens.

Through collaboration with contractors and stakeholders, PWD Kerala strives to meet the diverse needs of both urban and rural communities, while adhering to environmental standards and modern engineering practices. For more information on our projects and services, please contact us or explore our website.
